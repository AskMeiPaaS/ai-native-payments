Indian Payment Routing Decision Matrix — Risk Profile, Amount & Trusted Partner Based

This document guides the PaSS Orchestrator Agent in recommending the optimal payment channel based on three factors: (1) transaction amount, (2) user/counterparty risk profile, and (3) trusted partner tier.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 1: AMOUNT-BASED PRIMARY CHANNEL RECOMMENDATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

₹0 – ₹500:
  Primary: UPI Lite (instant, no PIN needed)
  Fallback: UPI, Cash
  Notes: UPI Lite maximum ₹500/txn, ₹2,000 wallet balance

₹501 – ₹10,000:
  Primary: UPI (instant, zero cost)
  Fallback: NEFT (if UPI unavailable), Cash (discouraged for business)
  Notes: Section 40A(3) — business cash above ₹10,000 not deductible

₹10,001 – ₹50,000:
  Primary: UPI (instant, zero cost)
  Fallback: NEFT (half-hourly batch)
  Avoid: Cash (PAN required above ₹50K, tax implications)
  Notes: Most banks support UPI up to ₹1L

₹50,001 – ₹1,00,000:
  Primary: UPI (within standard ₹1L limit)
  Fallback: NEFT (no amount cap, free online)
  Avoid: Cash (PAN mandatory, SFT reporting applies)
  Notes: PAN documentation needed for cash in this range

₹1,00,001 – ₹2,00,000:
  Primary: NEFT (free online, no upper limit)
  Alternative: UPI (only for verified merchants — ₹2L P2M limit)
  Avoid: Cash (ILLEGAL above ₹2L under Section 269ST)
  Notes: Cash receipt above ₹2L attracts 100% penalty

₹2,00,001 – ₹5,00,000:
  Primary: NEFT (free online, batch settlement)
  Alternative: UPI (only for tax/IPO/hospital/education — ₹5L enhanced limit)
  Business urgent: RTGS (minimum ₹2L, real-time)
  Prohibited: Cash

₹5,00,001 – ₹25,00,000:
  Primary: RTGS (real-time, finality guaranteed)
  Alternative: NEFT (if non-urgent)
  Notes: AML screening mandatory above ₹10L

Above ₹25,00,000:
  Primary: RTGS (real-time gross settlement, no upper limit)
  Notes: Multi-level authorization, compliance officer review for very large amounts
  Mandatory: AML/CFT screening, source-of-funds documentation

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 2: RISK PROFILE MODIFIER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Risk profiles modify the base channel recommendation:

LOW RISK PROFILE (Behavioral Score ≥ 0.95):
- Apply standard amount-based routing above
- Auto-approve within trusted partner limits
- Minimal additional verification
- Suitable for: established customers, repeat transactions, known patterns

MEDIUM RISK PROFILE (Behavioral Score 0.80 – 0.94):
- Apply standard routing but add monitoring flags
- Reduce auto-approve thresholds by 50%
- Enable extended audit logging
- Suitable for: regular customers with occasional anomalies

HIGH RISK PROFILE (Behavioral Score 0.60 – 0.79):
- Downgrade channel: prefer NEFT over UPI (settlement delay allows intervention)
- HITL review for transactions above ₹25,000
- Enhanced device and geo-fence verification
- Block UPI for new beneficiaries until profile improves
- Suitable for: customers with anomalies, new device, geo-mismatch

CRITICAL RISK PROFILE (Behavioral Score < 0.60):
- HITL mandatory for ALL transactions regardless of amount
- Block high-value channels (RTGS, high-value NEFT)
- Limit UPI to ₹10,000 per transaction
- Recommend customer re-verification (KYC refresh)
- Mandatory STR filing if suspicious patterns detected

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 3: TRUSTED PARTNER TIER ADJUSTMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Partner tiers provide auto-approval thresholds that override HITL escalation:

TIER 1 — ESTABLISHED PARTNER:
Criteria: 50+ successful transactions, 1+ year relationship, zero fraud incidents, KYC current
  UPI auto-approve: up to ₹1,00,000
  NEFT auto-approve: up to ₹5,00,000
  RTGS auto-approve: up to ₹10,00,000
  Cheque auto-accept: up to ₹5,00,000
  Cash auto-accept: up to ₹50,000

TIER 2 — REGULAR PARTNER:
Criteria: 10–49 transactions, 3+ months, <1% dispute rate, KYC current
  UPI auto-approve: up to ₹50,000
  NEFT auto-approve: up to ₹2,00,000
  RTGS auto-approve: up to ₹5,00,000
  Cheque auto-accept: up to ₹2,00,000
  Cash auto-accept: up to ₹20,000

TIER 3 — NEW PARTNER:
Criteria: <10 transactions or <3 months relationship
  UPI auto-approve: up to ₹25,000
  NEFT auto-approve: up to ₹1,00,000
  RTGS: HITL required for all transactions
  Cheque: HITL required above ₹25,000
  Cash: HITL required above ₹10,000

UNVERIFIED / HIGH RISK PARTNER:
Criteria: No transaction history, failed KYC, or prior fraud flags
  ALL channels: HITL mandatory for any transaction above ₹5,000
  RTGS: blocked
  Cheque: blocked
  Cash: limited to ₹2,000

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 4: COMBINED DECISION RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Rule 1 — Legal Compliance Override:
  Cash above ₹2,00,000: ALWAYS REJECT (Section 269ST)
  Cash deposit above ₹50,000 without PAN: ALWAYS REJECT
  RTGS below ₹2,00,000: ALWAYS REJECT (below minimum)

Rule 2 — Risk × Amount Escalation:
  If (risk_score < 0.80) AND (amount > ₹50,000): mandatory HITL
  If (risk_score < 0.60): mandatory HITL regardless of amount
  If first_transaction AND amount > ₹25,000: mandatory HITL

Rule 3 — Channel Availability Fallback:
  If UPI unavailable → NEFT → RTGS (if above ₹2L) → Cheque (last resort)
  If NEFT unavailable → RTGS (if above ₹2L) → UPI (if within limit)
  System downtime: queue for retry, notify user of expected delay

Rule 4 — Speed Priority:
  Urgent + ₹2L+: RTGS (real-time, minutes)
  Urgent + under ₹1L: UPI (instant)
  Non-urgent any amount: NEFT (up to 2 hours)
  Paper trail needed: Cheque (T+1 clearing)

Rule 5 — Cost Optimization:
  All digital channels: zero cost for savings account online transactions
  RTGS offline/branch: ₹25–₹50 per transaction
  Cheque: free but dishonour penalty risk ₹150–₹750
  Cash: hidden costs (PAN compliance, reporting, tax implications)
